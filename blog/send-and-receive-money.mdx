HaloPesa enable users to send and receive money between HaloPesa and HaloPesa, also to/from other mobile financial service providers. HaloPesa transactions with MPesa, Airtel Money, TPesa, TigoPesa and EzyPesa users are so as easy as transacting with other HaloPesa users 24/7 with minimum charges.

⦁	**Dial** \*150*88#

⦁	Select “1” to send money

⦁	Select “1” send to HaloPesa

⦁	Select 2-6 to send money to other networks
