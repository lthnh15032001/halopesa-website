#### 1.	Send Money
###### *** 1. P2P and Cashout Charges: ***

###### *** 1.1.	Send to wallet ***

HaloPesa allows users to send and receive money not only to HaloPesa users but also to all mobile financial service providers in Tanzania as M-Pesa, Airtel Money, Tigo Pesa, Ezy Pesa and T- Pesa.
